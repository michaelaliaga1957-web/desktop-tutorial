ESPLENDOR DETAILING

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Esplendor Detailing ("we," "us") is a mobile car detailing business in Houston, Texas. This policy explains what information we collect when you use esplendordetailing.com, how we use it, and your choices. We keep this simple and we don't sell your data.

Information We Collect

When you submit our booking form, we collect the details you provide: your name, phone number, email address (optional), vehicle, selected package and add-ons, preferred date and time, service address, and any notes.

We also collect standard analytics data automatically (such as pages visited and general device/browser information) through Google Analytics, to understand how the site is used.

How We Use It

  • To contact you and confirm your appointment (by call, text, WhatsApp, or email)
  • To provide and schedule your detailing service at your location
  • To send you a booking confirmation and, if you opt in, service reminders or review requests
  • To understand and improve our website and marketing

Who We Share It With

We use a small number of trusted service providers to run the business. Your booking information may be processed by:

  • Google — booking data is stored in Google Sheets and emails are sent via Gmail; website analytics via Google Analytics and Google Ads.
  • Formspree — a form-delivery service that forwards booking submissions to us as a backup.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

Cookies & Analytics

Our site uses Google Analytics (and Google Ads conversion measurement) which set cookies to measure traffic and ad performance. You can block cookies in your browser settings or opt out of Google Analytics using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Retention & Your Choices

We keep booking information only as long as needed to serve you and maintain our business records. You may request a copy of your information, or ask us to delete it, at any time — just contact us and we'll take care of it.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
