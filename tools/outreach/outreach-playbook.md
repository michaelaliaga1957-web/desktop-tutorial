# Esplendor Detailing — Hyperlocal Outreach Playbook

Goal: your first 10–20 paying customers, fast, for ~$0. These are the channels that actually produce first customers for a mobile detailer. Assets included: `assets/print/flyer-en.png`, `flyer-es.png` (4×6, print-ready), `qr.png`.

Track everything: the flyer QR links to `esplendordetailing.com/?utm_source=flyer` so flyer-driven visits show up in Google Analytics. Log where every lead came from in your Bookings sheet.

---

## 1. Flyers / door hangers (highest first-customer ROI)

**Where:** Target affluent, single-family neighborhoods with driveways (Katy — Cinco Ranch/Cross Creek; Sugar Land — Telfair/Riverstone; Pearland — Shadow Creek). Also **apartment complex** notice boards (ask the office — some allow it) and **windshields** in complex lots.
**Do NOT** put flyers in mailboxes — that's a federal offense. Door handles, windshields, community boards only.
**How many:** Print 200–300 to start (~$30–50 at a local print shop or Vistaprint). Hand out 50/day.
**Which version:** ES flyer for predominantly Hispanic neighborhoods, EN elsewhere. Bring both.

## 3. Facebook groups (join 5–8, post the same neighbor-tone copy)

Search and join: "Katy Buy Sell Trade", "Sugar Land Community", "Pearland TX", "[your neighborhood] neighbors", "Houston Latino Business", "Houston Moms". Read each group's rules — many have a specific day for promos. Post as a neighbor, not an ad (use the Nextdoor copy above). Reply to every comment fast.

**Facebook Marketplace:** List under Services → "Mobile Car Detailing — We Come To You" with a flyer image + the $25 offer. Renew weekly.

---

## 4. The 20-warm-contacts text (do this today)

Text 20 people you know personally:
> "Hey [name]! I just launched my mobile detailing business — I come to you and detail your car in your driveway. Doing $25 off for my first clients. Want me to make your car look brand new? 🚗✨ esplendordetailing.com"

Warm contacts → first real jobs → first real reviews. This is the fastest path to breaking zero.

---

## 5. After every job (non-negotiable — this compounds)

1. Take a **before + after photo** of the same spot (paint, wheels, or interior).
2. Post it to Instagram/TikTok as a Reel that day (@esplendordetailing).
3. In your Bookings sheet, set the row Status to **Paid** → the Review Engine texts/emails them the review link automatically.

Every job = 1 piece of content + 1 review request. That's the flywheel.
