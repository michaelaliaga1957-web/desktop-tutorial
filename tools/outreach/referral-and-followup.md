# Referral + Follow-Up Templates (copy-paste)

Every customer is a lead source and every lead is a future customer. These sequences turn one job into the next.

---

## 🔁 REFERRAL — offer it right after a great job (in person + text)

**In person (say it):**
> "Really glad you're happy with it. If you know anyone who'd want the same — friends, neighbors, coworkers — send them my way. You'll both get $10 off your next detail. I'll take great care of them."

**Text (send that evening):**
> Thanks again [Name]! 🙌 If you refer a friend, you BOTH get $10 off your next detail — just have them mention your name when they book. Book link: esplendordetailing.com

**How to track:** in the Bookings sheet Notes column, write "referred by [Name]". Apply $10 to both next details.

---

## ⚡ SPEED-TO-LEAD — reply to every new lead in under 5 minutes

New leads come in by form (you get the email), text, WhatsApp, or DM. Reply FAST — speed is the #1 predictor of closing a local service lead.

**First reply (text/DM):**
> Hi [Name]! Thanks for reaching out to Esplendor Detailing 🚗 I'd love to detail your [vehicle]. For a [package] it's $[price], and I come right to you. I have openings [day] — want me to lock one in? (No payment to book.)

---

## ⏰ NO-BOOK FOLLOW-UP — lead asked but didn't book

**+3 hours (same day):**
> Hey [Name], still happy to get your [vehicle] looking brand new. Want me to hold a spot for you this week? 🚗

**+24 hours:**
> Hi [Name]! Just following up — I have a couple openings left [day/day]. With the founding offer it's $[discounted price], and you don't pay until you're thrilled. Want me to book you in?

**+3 days (last touch):**
> Hey [Name], I'll stop bugging you 😄 — but the $25 SuperWash founding offer is still good if you'd like to grab it. Text me anytime: 713-501-0461.

---

## 📅 APPOINTMENT CONFIRMATION + REMINDER

**On booking (you send after confirming):**
> You're booked! ✅ [Day] [Date] between [time window]. I'll come to [address] with everything — just have the vehicle accessible. Text me if anything changes. — Michael, Esplendor Detailing

**Day before (reminder):**
> Reminder: your detail is tomorrow [Date] between [time] 🚗 I'll text when I'm on the way. Anything you want me to focus on? See you then!

**On my way:**
> On my way to you now, [Name] — see you in about [X] min! 🚗✨

## ⭐ POST-JOB (this is automated by the Review Engine, but here's the manual text for phone-only customers)

**Right after payment (in person):** "If you're happy, a quick Google review means the world to a new business like mine — takes 30 seconds. I'll text you the link."

**Text (send within the hour):**
> Thank you [Name]! 🙌 Hope your [vehicle] is looking amazing. If you have 30 sec, a Google review helps my new business so much: https://g.page/r/CViSYen6e8RIEBM/review — and remember, refer a friend and you both get $10 off! 🤝
