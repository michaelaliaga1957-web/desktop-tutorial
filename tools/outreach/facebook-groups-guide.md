# Facebook Groups — Exact Step-by-Step (find, join, post)

Facebook Groups are where Houston locals ask "who does ___?" Your job: get into the right groups and either (a) answer people already asking, or (b) post your offer where rules allow.

---

## STEP 1 — Find & join groups (20 minutes, one time)

1. Open Facebook → tap the **search bar** at the top.
2. Type a group name from the list below → tap **Search** → tap the **"Groups"** tab (filter).
3. On each result, tap **Join**. If it asks 1–3 questions, answer them (see Step 2).
4. Join **15–20 groups** total. Aim for a mix of the 3 types below.

## STEP 2 — If a group asks join questions
Answer simply and honestly:
- *"Why do you want to join?"* → "I live in the area and run a local mobile detailing business — happy to follow all group rules."
- *"Do you agree to the rules?"* → Yes.
- *"Are you a local business?"* → Yes, Esplendor Detailing (mobile car detailing).

Approval can take minutes to a day. Move on and come back.

---

## STEP 3 — READ each group's rules first (2 min per group)
Tap the group → **"About"** or **"Featured"** → find the rules. Look for:
- **"Promo day"** — many groups only allow business posts on a certain day (e.g., "Self-Promo Sunday"). Post then.
- **"No self-promotion"** — do NOT post an ad there. Instead, only **reply to people asking** for recommendations (Step 5).

Breaking rules = banned. Following them = trusted.

## STEP 5 — The move that actually gets customers TODAY (do this in EVERY group)

Most jobs come from replying to people **already asking**, not from your own posts.

1. In each group, tap the **magnifying glass / "Search this group"**.
2. Search: `detail`, then `car cleaning`, then `mobile detail`.
3. Anyone who posted "who does mobile detailing?" in the last few weeks → **comment**:
> "Hey! I do mobile detailing — Esplendor Detailing. I come to you, full detail is $129.99 and I'm doing $25 off for new clients. Text me 713-501-0461 and I'll take great care of your car 🚗"
4. Also reply to it and **send them a DM** (Message).
5. **Turn on notifications** for your best 5 groups (tap the group → bell icon → "All posts") so you see new "recommendation" requests fast and reply first. First to reply usually wins.

---

## Daily rhythm (30 min/day)
- Post in 2–3 promo-allowed groups (rotate; don't spam the same group daily).
- Search 5 groups for "detail" and reply to anyone asking.
- Reply to any new "who does ___" posts within minutes.
- Log every lead in your Bookings sheet (source = "FB group [name]").

## Reality check
Groups are a slow drip — expect a few leads over 1–2 weeks, not instant. The FASTER lever is direct: text your 20 warm contacts, and post in **Recommendation groups** where demand is active. Do both.
